My Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Combine the flour and butter.
  3. Combine the dough and filling.
  4. Roll out the dough and spread the filling.
  5. Roll up the dough and cut it into 12 pieces.
  6. Place the rolls in a baking dish and let them rise for 1 hour.
  7. Bake the rolls in a preheated oven at 375Â°F for 18 minutes.
